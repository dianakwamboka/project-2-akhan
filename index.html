akan names

Akan names originate from Ghana.

Names in ghana are given in regards to the day a child is born.

Male names:

  1. Sunday: Kwasi
  2. Monday: Kwadwo
  3. Tuesday: Kwabena
  4. Wednesday: Kwaku
  5. Thursday: Yaw
  6. Friday: Kofi
  7. Saturday: Kwame
Female names:
  1. Sunday: Akosua
  2. Monday: Adwoa
  3. Tuesday: Abenaa
  4. Wednesday: Akua
  5. Thursday: Yaa
  6. Friday: Afua
  7. Saturday: Ama
